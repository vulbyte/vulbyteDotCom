mod help

a general resource for mods of vulbyte streams to use
  • Be Fair & Consistent: Apply rules equally to everyone. No favorites.
  • Enforce the Rules: Immediately address spam, hate speech, threats, and harassment.
  • Use the 3-Strike System (Suggested): Use a brief Warning/Timeout for minor offenses, a Longer Timeout for repeat offenses, and a Ban for persistent rule-breaking or severe harassment.
  • Keep the Vibe Positive: Encourage good discussion and fun. Remove negativity that derails the chat.
  • Don't Argue: Do not engage in back-and-forth arguments with trolls or rule-breakers. Action, then move on.
  • Mute Backseat Gaming: Remove unsolicited "spoilers" or commands unless the streamer explicitly asks for help.
  • Trust Your Team: When in doubt about a ban, confer privately with other mods or the streamer.
  • Know When to DM: If a situation is complex, message the streamer or the mod group privately. Do not debate mod actions in public chat.
